Cake Recipe

Cake is a concoction of perfectly curated ingredients that help deliver a heavenly taste upon our taste buds.

Cake is a beautiful, fudgey treat and dessert that is thoroughly enjoyed all throughout the world!

Ingredients:

  • Vegetable Oil
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Sour Cream
  • Baking Powder
  • Baking Soda
  • Vanilla

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Whisk cocoa powder and 1.5 cups boiling water in bowl till smooth
  3. Add flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in large bowl and whisk until combined
  4. Add eggs, vegetable oil, sour cream, and vanilla and beat with mixer on medium speed until smooth
  5. Divide batter between pans. Bake until toothpick inside comes out clean
  6. Remove parchment and enjoy
