Omelette Recipe

Whether it is the morning, afternoon, or just before bed- and omelette is always a safe bet when you're hungry.

These delicious creations are also packed with essential nutrients for your body!

Ingredients:

  • 1 Egg
  • 1 Tsp Oil

Steps:

  1. Preheat pan to medium
  2. Gently pour 1 tsp of oil
  3. Add your egg
  4. Whisk until ready to serve
  5. Enjoy
